Our Lead Expert

Dr. Emily Carter, PhD

Lead Nutrition Scientist

Dr. Carter holds a PhD in Nutrition Science from Stanford University and has spent over 15 years researching the relationship between diet, metabolism, and longevity. Frustrated by the amount of misinformation in the health and wellness space, she co-founded Wellness Verified to provide a resource that people could rely on for credible, science-backed advice.

Her work focuses on translating complex scientific data into practical, actionable strategies that help people improve their health in a sustainable way. When she's not analyzing research papers, she enjoys hiking and experimenting with new healthy recipes.
