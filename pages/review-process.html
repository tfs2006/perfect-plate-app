Our Methodology

The "Trust Stack" Framework

To ensure our reviews are fair, balanced, and genuinely useful, we evaluate every product using our proprietary "Trust Stack" methodology. This framework is designed to look beyond marketing claims and assess products on what truly matters.

1. Scientific Evidence & Formulation

Our analysis begins with the product's formula. We scrutinize each ingredient and cross-reference it with high-quality scientific literature from reputable sources like PubMed, the National Institutes of Health, and peer-reviewed journals. We ask critical questions: Is the ingredient included at a clinically effective dose? Does the evidence support the product's claims? We prioritize products with transparent, evidence-backed formulations.

2. Safety & Quality Verification

A great formula is meaningless if the product isn't safe or pure. We investigate whether a product is manufactured in a facility that adheres to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). Most importantly, we give strong preference to products that undergo voluntary third-party testing from independent labs (like NSF, USP, or ISO 17025 accredited labs) to verify that what's on the label is actually in the bottle—and nothing else.

3. Real User Experience

To understand how a product performs in the real world, we conduct a comprehensive analysis of customer reviews from multiple verified-purchase platforms. We look for consistent themes, both positive and negative, regarding a product's effectiveness, taste, side effects, and overall user satisfaction. This helps provide a balanced view beyond the curated testimonials on a product's own website.

4. Brand Reputation & Value

Finally, we assess the company behind the product. Does the brand have a history of transparency and good customer service? Do they offer a fair money-back guarantee? We also evaluate the product's price relative to its formulation and competitors to determine its overall value. We believe a trustworthy product should come from a trustworthy brand.

Our Commitment to You

This rigorous process allows us to provide recommendations with confidence. Our goal is to be the resource we wished we had on our own health journeys—one that is clear, honest, and always on the side of the reader.
